How We Work

We are problem solvers. We are partners. We are collaborators. We believe the best software is that which changes often. We are transparent, honest, and hard-working. We want to partner with you to make a difference. That’s our process.

Project Management

Building a software product can feel like throwing a dart at a dartboard. You feel some anxiety over the risk of the dart landing in the wrong place. But that’s okay, because in reality, software is more akin to a living organism than an abstract entity. That means iteration (what we call change) is something that’s planned for and expected. The best products are built for people, and with people in mind—that means the sooner we can get a product in front of people, the sooner we can understand how the product needs to look and work.

Research, Design & Development

Our design process is extensive and flows directly into development. In fact, design and development work so hand-in-hand that they are almost the same thing at RoaringSteed. This is intentional, even to the point of our developers’ being involved in design decisions and vice-versa. Both design and development are essential to building a successful product from beginning to end.

Pricing & Investment
